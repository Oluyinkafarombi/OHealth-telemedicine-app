Where Can I Download The Application

OHealth for Users and Odoctor for Doctors is Avaialable on the google playstore for download, You can also download the application from this website by clicking on the download button.

How Do I Talk To The Same Doctor Again

In the case where you'll like to have the same doctor attend to you, User will contact any of the support channels (Phone Call, WhatsApp, Text Message or send an email), with necessary information such as Doctors Name, Last time the Doctor attended to you. the admin then help you schedule a meeting with the doctor and inform you via email, whatsApp or Text Message.

How Do I Load My Account

Users Can load their account by selecting the My account button from the application Dashboard or the side menu, select Fund My Account enter any amount, then fill other necessary details to complete Transaction.

How does Ohealth save me time and money?

We save you time and money by making it possible to get great care without having to see an Doctor. This means you don't need to spend ages on the telephone trying to book an appointment, to travel to and from the doctor's office, or to sit in a waiting room.

Is this a safe way to get care?

It is, as long as you answer our questions fully and truthfully and you read the important information we provide throughout our app and website,.
